The building blocks of Envision

A few simple ideas work together to turn your big goals into a life you actually live. Here's everything you'll meet inside the app.

Visions

A goal with a deadline. Tell Envision what you want to achieve and when, and it breaks the goal into manageable daily tasks — so you always know your next step.

Rhythms

Recurring habits you want to build — pushups, miles, prayer, reading. Set a weekly target and watch your consistency map fill in, day by day.

Memory Hooks

One sentence and a photo about your day, pinned to the calendar. A year from now you'll remember not just what you did, but who you were.

To-Dos

Loose tasks that don't belong to a Vision — quick errands and one-off items. Keep them in one place so nothing slips through the cracks.

Calendar

Your whole life in one view. Syncs with Apple Calendar to show events, tasks, and memories together — so your day always makes sense at a glance.

Consistency Map

A visual streak of every day you showed up for a Rhythm. The grid fills in as you build momentum — a simple, motivating picture of your progress.

Privacy Policy

Privacy first

Your goals and habits are personal. We're built with privacy by design so you can focus on what matters most.

Your data is stored on your device and synced privately via your iCloud account.
Calendar access stays on-device and is never sent to our servers.
Photos you attach to Memory Hooks are not uploaded to a server.
Notifications are optional and never include your content.
We don't sell your data. Ever.

Envision ("we," "our," or "the app") is built with your privacy as a priority. This policy explains what data the app handles and how. By using Envision, you agree to the practices described here.

Information stored on your device and in iCloud

When you use Envision, the content you create is saved on your device and, if you have iCloud enabled, synced through your personal Apple iCloud account so it stays available across your Apple devices. This includes:

  • Goals and tasks you create (Visions, Rhythms, To-Dos, Schedule Blocks)
  • Journal entries and notes (Memory Hooks)
  • Photos you attach to Memory Hooks
  • Your in-app preferences and settings

This information is stored on your device and in your own iCloud account. It is not transmitted to or stored on servers operated by us. iCloud syncing is handled by Apple under Apple's iCloud terms and privacy policy, and we do not have access to the contents of your iCloud data.

Calendar access

If you choose to connect Apple Calendar, Envision accesses your calendar events so it can display them alongside your tasks and help you schedule your day. This calendar information is used only within the app to power these features and is not sent to our servers. You can revoke calendar access at any time in your device's Settings.

Photos

If you attach a photo to a Memory Hook, the app accesses your photo library only to let you select that image. The photo is stored within the app on your device and in your iCloud account. We do not upload your photos to our servers.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, Envision uses your device's notification system to alert you. Notifications are optional, do not include sensitive content, and can be turned off at any time in your device settings.

Analytics and third parties

The app may use Apple's standard, privacy-preserving tools (such as aggregate crash and performance reporting through App Store Connect) to help us fix bugs and improve stability. This information is aggregated and does not personally identify you. We do not use third-party advertising or tracking, and we do not sell your data.

Data retention and deletion

Because your data lives on your device and in your iCloud account, you remain in control of it. You can delete individual Visions, Rhythms, To-Dos, and Memory Hooks within the app at any time. Deleting the app removes its data from your device, and you can manage or remove associated iCloud data through your device's iCloud settings.

Children's privacy

Envision is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

As Envision adds new features, this policy may be updated to reflect any changes in how data is handled, and the "last updated" date will change accordingly.
